Eccomerce

Overview

All-in-one

Apped is a complete solution for creating an online store. It's designed for use on mobile devices (You also get a desktop version of the shop) and has all the features you need to run a successful eCommerce business.

Universal access

Apped is available as native apps for iOS, Android, and Huawei, as well as a PWA (Progressive Web App) that works on any device with a web browser. This means you can reach all your customers, no matter what device they're using.

ASO and SEO

Apped is listed on the Apple and Google app stores, and can also be found through search engines like Google. This helps you get noticed and increases your presence on all sales channels.

Easy payments

Apped partners with PayFast, Ozow, and other payment providers to make it easy for customers to make purchases. Customers can use one-click payments or saved payment methods.

Catalog eCommerce

Apped allows you to easily manage your store's catalog, including adding and organizing products, updating stock levels, setting prices, and more. You can also group products together into collections to make them easier to browse.

Marketing tools

Apped includes tools to help you promote your store and reach out to customers. You can send push notifications, use email templates, and offer discounts and promotions.

Product Management

Product Thumbnail

Your product thumbnails are automatically generated from your default image. You can easily change it by selecting a different image and setting it as your thumbnail source. You can have multiple sliding images per product.

Variants

Quickly create variations of your products, such as different sizes, colors, materials, or any other variations. Each variant has its own characteristics (images, reference, stock, price). You can create up to 100 variations per product.

Featured Products

You have the option to set specific products as featured products, which will be displayed on the home page of your shop.

Stock Management

Easily manage your stock levels from the admin interface. For each product and its variant, you can set a starting stock. After each sale, the stock will be updated to reflect the quantity sold. You also have the option to set an unlimited stock.

Out-of-Stock

Keep your out-of-stock products displayed in your catalog with an "out-of-stock" label. This way, your customers still have access to your full catalog, even if a product is temporarily unavailable.

Eccomerce

Back Office

Shop Information

Set all your shop information in one place, including contact details (address, email, phone), shop name, metric standards (weight and currency), and social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.). You can also add banner images to your shop and link them to a specific URL or tab. You can even manage your shop on your mobile device.

Categories

Create shopping categories and go as deep as three tiers, such as parent category, subcategory, and sub-subcategory.

Custom URL

Link your domain name to your back office if you don't want to access it through the app.

Check Out Process

Auto Login

Your customers are automatically authenticated after their first connection. They are recognized as soon as they launch the app, making it easier and faster to make a purchase on your shop.

Permanent Cart

With auto login, the cart becomes permanent. Customers can add products to the cart, leave the app, and come back later to finish their order. They will find the same cart waiting for them, similar to Amazon's shopping cart.

Order Details

Changing the Status of an Order

Easily change the status of an order with just one click. This will generate an automatic email and push notification to the customer, keeping them informed of the progress of their order. You can also change the status of an order from your mobile device. Orders are stored on the backend, allowing you to export or print orders to PDF if needed.

Order Tracking

Customers can track their orders through the mobile app or online web store/PWA.

Change Shipping Address

You can change the shipping address of an order if the customer reports an error or if you notice an error yourself.
